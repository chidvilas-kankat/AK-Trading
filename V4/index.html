§01 · The villain

You weren't gullible.
You were targeted.

India's retail traders lost ₹1.81 lakh crore in F&O in FY24. SEBI itself confirms ~9 in 10 lose. The gurus selling the dream are a big part of why — and the regulators are finally cracking down. ₹546 crore has been impounded from rogue "academies" in just the last 18 months.

You know the playbook by now, because you've probably been on the receiving end of it:

  • 01—

    The "guru" with a rented supercar and a screenshot of one winning trade out of fifty.

  • 02—

    The "educator" who's really just selling a paid tips/signals group on the side.

  • 03—

    The course that's 90% theory, 10% useful, and 100% followed by an "advanced" course you also "need".

  • 04—

    The promise of "₹X a day, guaranteed", aimed straight at your hope.

It works because losing money is painful and hope is powerful. The hype industry isn't selling you trading — it's selling you the absence of that pain. That's why we call them what they are.

§03 · The methodology

The Honest Method.

Substance behind the stance. Four principles, deliberately the opposite of the hype playbook.

PRINCIPLE 01 ↗

Independence over dependence

You leave able to trade on your own. No subscription you're trapped in, no daily call you can't function without. The goal of the course is that you don't need us.

PRINCIPLE 02 ↗

Risk before reward

Capital preservation is taught first — position sizing, stop placement, drawdown control — because the people still trading next year are the people who didn't blow up this year.

PRINCIPLE 03 ↗

Process over predictions

A written plan with rules — entries, exits, invalidation, max loss — replaces "feelings" and other people's tips. The market doesn't reward predictions; it rewards process.

PRINCIPLE 04 ↗

Honesty over hype

The odds, the losses and the disclaimers are front-and-centre, not buried in 6-point text on page 14. If you can't face the truth of the market, you can't trade it.

How do I know you're not just another guru? +

Fair question — start with what we refuse to do: no tips, no guarantees, no profit screenshots, no upsell ladder, no Telegram VIP group. Then check the credentials: NISM-Certified RA, real registration number on every page. Then come to the free masterclass and judge for yourself before you pay a single rupee. If we're a guru, you'll smell it within ten minutes.

Do you have a private tips or signals group? +

No. That is exactly what we are against — and it is illegal for an educator under SEBI's Research Analyst regulations. Our community is for Q&A and accountability only. We will remove you from it for posting buy/sell calls. The whole point of this school is that you don't need anyone's calls.

Why no profit screenshots on the website? +

Because they're meaningless as proof — trivially fakeable in ten seconds with any broker's mobile app — and they're the calling card of the people we're the opposite of. We prove value through teaching, not flexing. If you want screenshots, the rest of the internet has plenty. We'd rather you didn't come to us for that reason.

Do you guarantee I'll make money? +

Never. ~9 in 10 traders lose money in F&O, by SEBI's own data. Nobody can guarantee market outcomes — and anyone who claims otherwise is either lying or about to be in trouble. We teach the discipline of the few — honestly, with the odds stated up front. What happens to your account is between you, your discipline and the market.

I'm a complete beginner. Is this for me? +

Yes — and possibly more so than for the half-burned mid-stage trader. Beginners are the people the hype industry targets hardest, and the ones it damages most. We start from the absolute fundamentals: how an exchange works, what a lot is, why options decay. If a term confuses you, you raise your hand. No question is "too basic" in this school.
